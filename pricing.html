Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you support?

We prefer being paid in fiat. However, we understand that you might have your reservations against fiat money and we're open to discuss a split between fiat & the crypto equivalent of the bill.

How soon can you start?

We usually start immediately upon signing the agreement. We spend the first few days getting to know your project & goals, after that we get down to work.

Can I see more of your work?

Absolutely! You can see our work here, here and here. Alternatively, reach out to us and we'll love to walk you through some of our favorite projects!

Do you offer discounts?

Sure! For long-term projects, we can discuss a fair pricing which works for you. It depends on the scope of the work so we'll need to get to know your project first.

Will you help us promoting my product as well?

We can't. Due to the nature of our work, we tend to work with competing platforms & projects and We try to be fair to our clients. While we don't promote/endorse them, we always make sure we're invested in the project to have some skin in the game!

Do you also do development?

Absolutely! While it's not our personal expertise, we do collaborate with some of the finest development agencies to bring your project to life. Alternative, we'll also be happy to collaborate with an agency of your choice.
